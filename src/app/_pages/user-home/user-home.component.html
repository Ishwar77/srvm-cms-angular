The distinguishing features of the Mandiram

Seeking to give expression to Swami Vivekanandaâ€™s ideal of "Man-making education", the Sri Ramakrishna Vidyarthi Mandiram was started in 1943. It is a modern students home in the setting of an ancient Brahmacharya Ashrama. The aim of the Mandiram is to supplement the academic education of the university by imparting training conducive to an all-round growth of the students. The objective is to provide a suitable home for deserving boys from outside Bangalore, who want to benefit from the care and guidance of the Mandiram.

  • An entirely non-communal and non-political outlook and atmosphere

  • Facilities provided for building up character and spiritual life of the students

  • Special stress on physical fitness and games

  • Encouragement and opportunities for the development of talent
